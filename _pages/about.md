I am a PhD student in Computer Science at [Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin](https://www.hu-berlin.de/de), working at the [Software Engineering](https://www.informatik.hu-berlin.de/en/forschung-en/gebiete/se/se) chair under the supervision of [Prof. Dr. Lars Grunske](https://www.informatik.hu-berlin.de/de/Members/lars-grunske)). My research explores the connections between code, tests, and documentation. I focus on generation-based techniques that use these relationships to detect and fix inconsistencies, aiming to make software more reliable and usable.
